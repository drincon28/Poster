RHYTHME
Web music rhythm assistant focused on kids between 4 and 12 years old with arduino multipad
Diana Paola Rincón - drincon28@uan.edu.co
Wilmer Rodríguez - wrodriguez462@uan.edu.co
Director:Wilson Forero - wilson.forero@uan.edu.co
Context

Music is one of the intelligences: H.G.
Rhythm is present everywhere, in our lives, his learning gives us advantages in multiple human activities.
Question

How could we implement a free portal to support rhythmic musical learning?
Problem

No training feedback without tutor

No accessible technology

Expensive tools

Loud percusion instruments

Boring methodology


Kids face these among other
problems when they are trying
to learn or practice their exercises.
Main Objective

Implement a web portal to learn musical rhythms, automonously, for children between 4 and 12 years old using modern web tools.
Objectives

Develop a site with modern web gaming tools for student's practice.

Improve the learning process with instant feedback on every hit the player success/miss.

Create a full media guide DIY to build a keyboard or an arduino multipad.

Deliver multimedia definitions about rhythm in a kids-friendly website .
